timefmt

Formats timestamps based on Golang’s time formatting.

Examples

    timefmt -input "2006-01-02" -output "01/02/2006" "2016-07-02"

Outputs 07/02/2016

    timefmt -input mysql -output RFC822  "2016-07-02 08:08:08"

Outputs 02 Jul 16 08:08 UTC
